Welcome to List.it!

What Is List.it?

List.it is a site where people can sell, trade or auction items that they own, or find items to buy or trade for! Users can easily upload images and add items to the site, which they can then use to create their own listings that they can easily manage. Your listings will become public for other users to see, and they can easily make offers or place bids on those listings.

What Features Does List.it Offer?


As A Seller...
  • Trade items for other user's items
  • Sell/auction items for cash
  • Group items together in listings
  • Easily manage listings
    •

    As A Buyer...
  • Place offers of items/cash on listings
  • Place bids on auctions
  • Send payments online through PayPal
  • Search for items using names/tags
    •

    Anyone...
  • Upload images and create items
  • Start conversations with other users
  • Create events and invite other users
  • Create a wishlist and search listings
    •

    But That's Not All...

    List.it has implemeted a PayPal payment system into the site so that users can make payments online to other users instead of in person! Receipts are automatically made for listings once they're completed, and allows users to easily make payments through them. The receipts allow users to retain a history of items they've sold and bought as well, so they can easily track where items and cash went for future reference.
