How to Edit Your Account

A user can edit their account by clicking on "Profile" in the navigation bar in the top right, and on the new page, there will be a button near the top right that says "Edit Account". Clicking on this button will take you to the form where you can edit your account. When editing your account, a PayPal email address and your first and last name are required. In this form, you can set whether you want users to invite you to events or have them message you. Check "Open to Invites?" if you would like to receive invitations or leave it unchecked to not receive them. Check "Open to Inquiries?" if you want users to message, leave unchecked if you do not want users to message you. Once you have finished making changes, click on "Update" to save the changes to your account.

There is also a reset password link on the form page if one needs to reset their password. Click on the link where you'll then be taken to a new page asking for the email you used to sign up with. Enter the email address and click the button to have a password reset email sent to the email you provided.

FAQs for Accounts

Can I change my username and/or email?

At the moment we are not allowing users to change their usernames and emails themselves. If a user does need to change them for whatever reason, send in a report detailing the changes you want and an admin will make the changes for you.
