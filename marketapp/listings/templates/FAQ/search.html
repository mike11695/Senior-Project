How to Search Listings

To search for listings that match a certain criteria, go to "All Listings" in the navigation bar in the top right corner, and in the drop down select "Search". Clicking on this link will take you to the search page where you can search for listings. There are several different paramaters you can use in your search, such as a name for a listing or possible words that are in a listing, the type of listing you want to find, the search radius of listings in an area, and lastly tags for images included in the items in the listing. You can combine the parameters as well to narrow down your search for listings as well in order to get more specific listings. Only active listings will appear in your search however. You will be able to favorite listings that you find in your searches as well by clicking on the star in the top right corner of every listing.

FAQs for Searching

Is there any other parameters I can include in my search?

At the moment there is not, but there may be some new ones added in the future, such as price ranges and names of items.
