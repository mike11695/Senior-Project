How to Create a Wishlist

Users have the option of creating a wishlist to organize items that they are searching for onto one page. In order to create your wishlist, go to the navigation bar in the top right and click on "Wishlist". If you haven't made a wishlist yet, you'll be taken to the form where you can make a wishlist. You will need to give your wishlist a title and a description, but you are not obligated to add items to it right away. Once you have added a title, description and items if you choose to add them now, click on "Create" to finish creating your wishlist. You'll then be taken to your wishlist's detail page, which you can then access later on by clicking on "Wishlist" in the navigation bar.

Please note that you can only make one wishlist per user account. All wishlist items you have will exist on the same wishlist.

How to Edit Your Wishlist

To edit your wishlist, click on "Wishlist" in the navigation bar in the top right to go to your wishlist's detail page. On this page, under the title and description of your wishlist there is a button that says "Edit Wishlist". Clicking on it will take you to the form where you can edit your wishlist. Remember that your wishlist must have at least a title and a description, but it does not need to contain any items. Once you are finished editing your wishlist, click on "Update" to save the changes made.

You can also quickly remove items from your wishlist. On your wishlist's detail page, each item that you have added to your wishlist will have a button that says "REMOVE". Clicking on this button will result in a confirmation dialogue popping up asking if you are sure you want to remove the item from your wishlist. If you are sure, click "Yes" and the item will be removed. If you need to add the item back at any time, you can easily do so by editing your wishlist.

How to Create a Wihslist Listing

Once you have added items to your wishlist, you'll be able to create search listings for them. Other users will be able to contact you if they see they have an item you seek and negotiate a trade or sale with you. In order to create a listing, hover over "My Listings" in the navigation bar in the top right and click on "Wishlist". On this page, there will be a button under the page header that says "Create Listing". Clicking on the button will take you to the form where you can create a wishlist listing using items on your wishlist. When creating your listing, make sure to give it a name, when you want your listing to end and any notes that you want to add. Your listing must also have at least one item to offer or a monetary amount. You may include both items and money to offer however. Once you have added all the required info, click on "Create" to finish creating your listing.

You can also quickly create a listing for an item on your wishlist by clicking on "LIST" under the item you want to list. This will take you to the same form as above but have the item you cicked on already preselected. However, note that even if you deselect the item you were creating the listing for it will still be added to your listing so make sure that you actually want to create a listing for the item before doing so.

FAQs for Wishlists

How many wishlists can I have?

Each user may only have one wishlist.

Can I delete my wishlist?

No, after creating your wishlist it will remain on the site permanently. If you have no need for it, simply remove any existing wishlist items on it so that no wishes remain.
