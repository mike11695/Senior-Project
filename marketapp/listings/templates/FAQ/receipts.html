How to View Receipts

A receipt is made when you complete one of your listings, or an offer you made is accepted or a bid you placed wins an auction. In order to view all of your active receipts, hover over "My Stuff" in the navigation bar in the top right corner and in the dropdown, select "Receipts". On the following page, you can see a list of all receipts, and in each container for each receipt, you can view information such as what items you offered or how much money you offered, and what items or cash amount you'll be receiving in return. A button is included where you can message the other user to discuss how you will exchange items and anything else you would like to discuss.

Making a Payment

If you had included a cash amount in an offer that was accepted or had a winning bid in an auction, you will need to send a payment to the other user. In order to do so, go to your list of receipts and for each receipt that includes a cash offer from you, there will be a button at the bottom of the container that says "Send Payment". Clicking on this button will take you to a form where you can select you payment type for PayPal. There are three options to choose from; "PayPal", "Pay Later" and "Debit or Credit Card".

If you would like to make a payment through PayPal using your PayPal account, click on "PayPal". In the following pop up box, enter your PayPal credentials and then proceed on. On the next page, you can review the amount you're paying and select the payment option you want to use to pay; your PayPal balance, your credit/debit card or your bank. Once you have reviewed all the information and selected your payment type, click on "Pay Now" and the transaction will be processed.

The second option for paying a user is "Pay Later". By using this option, you will not have to pay the full amount in one go, but will be able to make four payments towards the full amount over a six-week period. The user that you are making the payment to will still receive the payment when it's made, but you will be able to make payments when they are scheduled automatically by PayPal. Be advised that this can only be done with payment amounts between $30 and $600, but no fees or interest will charged as long as payments are made when scheduled.

The last option to pay with is "Debit or Credit Card". Use this option if you do not have a PayPal account to make payments with. In the form that pops up, you will be asked to enter your credit or debit cards details and your billing address information. The process is secured and PayPal will keep your information private, so you do not have to worry about other users having access to your details. If you use this option, you will be asked to create a PayPal account by providing an e-mail address and password. Once you have filled out all of the fields, click on "Create Account and Pay Now" to finalize the payment. You can then use the account you created in future purchases.

Once a payment has been made, you will receive a short summary of the transaction which can always be viewed again by clicking on "Payment Details" for the receipt in your receipt list view. The user you sent the payment to will also receive a notification to alert them that the payment has been made.

Deleting a Receipt

Once a payment has been made or no payment was included in the offer, you can delete a receipt. Be advised though that once you delete a receipt, you will no longer be able to view the information included on it. The other user will still be able to view the receipt however. It is advised that you should only delete receipts once both parties have received their items and/or payments and are happy.

FAQs for Receipts

What happens if I don't make a payment?

If you fail to make a payment to another user without communicating with them the reasons why, you will receive a strike to your account. If you receive too many strikes, you will be restricted from listing items and placing offers/bids on other listings, or you may have your account banned entirely.

What happens if I don't receive a payment?

If you don't receive a payment from another user and they have failed to communicate with you the reasons why, please submit a ticket for the issue to be reviewed, and action will be taken if appropriate. If you have already exchanged the items with the other user and received no payment, please submit a ticket as well.

I made a payment but received different items/items in worse quality than what was advertised, what should I do?

If you happen to be in this situation where you received different items than advertised or items in worse quality than advertised and you already had made a payment to the other user, please submit a ticket so that the issue can be reviewed, and action will be taken if appropriate.

I made a payment but never received the items I had paid for, what should I do?

If this happens and the seller refuses to communicate with you the issues on why you haven't received your items, please submit a ticket so that the issue can be reviewed, and action can be taken if approriate.
