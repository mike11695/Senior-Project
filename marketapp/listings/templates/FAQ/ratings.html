How to View Ratings

Ratings can be found on your profile detail page. In order to view your profile, click on "Profile" in the top right navigation bar. To view the ratings you have received from other users, scroll to the bottom of your profile and there will be a section for ratings. You can view up to 10 ratings at a time and can click on a page number below the ratings to view more.

How to Leave a Rating

In order to leave a rating for another user, you must have completed at least one exchange with a user, whether it be a trade or sale. When you have an offer accepted or a bid you placed wins an auction, a rating ticket related to you and the listing will be created. This also applies to the owner of the listing, but they'll receive their own ticket. This ticket allows you to leave a rating for the listing on the user's profile. When creating a rating, select the listing that you want to create the rating for. For the rating value, you can rate the other user on a scale of 1 to 5 for how well they did with communicating and exchanging goods with you. You can leave feedback as well, but it is optional. Once you have filled in the neccessary fields, simply click on "Post" to post the rating to the user's profile.

FAQs for Ratings

Can I edit/delete ratings I leave for other users?

No, you cannot edit or delete a rating after you create it. Please make sure that all the info is correct when you leave feedback as it is permanent once you post it.

Can I delete ratings other users left me?

No, you cannot delete ratings from other users from your profile. If a user leaves an inappropriate or inaccurate rating, please report it and an admin will look into it.

What happens if I receive many low ratings?

Nothing will happen if many users rate you low, but please try to improve on what they leave feedback on in order to improve and increase your reputation.
