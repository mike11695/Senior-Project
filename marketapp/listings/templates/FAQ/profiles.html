How to Edit Your Profile

Users upon creating their account are made a profile. You can view your profile at any time by clicking on "Profile" in the top right navigation bar. A user's profile will initially appear empty until the user edits it. On your profile detail page, there will be a button near the top right that says "Edit Profile". Clicking on it will take you to the edit form for your profile. As of now, you can add a biography and set your delivery drop off address if you are willing to deliver items. Once you have edited your profile to your liking, click on "Update" to save the changes.

Please note that when you update your profile, data will be saved regarding your location so that the site is able to show you listings in or around the area you live in. This is also done when you sign up for the first time. If you ever change your location, simply go to the edit form for profiles and click on "Update", and your location info will be updated automatically.

FAQs for Profiles

Is there a way to change my location myself?

No, there is no way to change your location by yourself. This is to avoid people abusing locations and creating false listings in areas they don't reside in.
